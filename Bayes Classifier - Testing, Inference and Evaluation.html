Simplify¶

$$ \frac{P(X \, | \, Spam ) \,(P(Spam))} {P(X)} > \frac{P(X \, | \, Ham ) \,(1 - P(Spam))} {P(X)}$$

Simplifies as

$$ P(X \, | \, Spam ) \,(P(Spam)) > P(X \, | \, Ham ) \,(1 - P(Spam))$$
